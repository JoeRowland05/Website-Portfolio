StoryBoard

My Storyboard from other work

My task was to create a storyboard in preparation for a 2D animation concerning the United Nations sustainability goals.

I picked 'life Under Water' due to personal interest in the ocean and a belief that this topic would really excel in regards to artistic style. My idea was to create a stop motion animation using drawings i have done focusing on how laziness or a lack of awareness of small changes we can make to our lives have a massive effect on the oceans ecosystem and how that has a knock on effect on us.

I will create and edit the animation using Adobe after effects in which i will utilise the opacity feature in combination with keyframes in order to make text or images disappear at different times. This also has to be accompanied by audio, i will add a voice over to the final composition with the words appearing on screen simaltaneously with them being spoken.
